Café au Nutella:

Home > Café au Nutella Recipe

Description

A pleasure every morning.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp. instant coffee
  • 2 sugar cubes
  • 3 tbsp. milk
  • 1 tsp. Nutella
  • Water, hot

Steps:

  1. Put instant coffee, sugar cubes and milk into a "latte macchiato" glas
  2. Fill the glass up with hot water
  3. Add Nutella and stir - DONE!
