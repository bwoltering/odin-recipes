Currysalat

Home > Currysalat Recipe

Description:

German delicacy with baloney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound baloney
  • 1 glass pickled cucumber
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pound ketchup
  • Currypowder
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut the baloney in stripes
  2. Cut some cucumbers (not all) in cubes
  3. Peel the onion and cut it in cubes
  4. Put everything in a bowl and add some water from the pickled cucumbers glass
  5. Add 1-2 tsp. mustard and the ketchup
  6. Stir and season with salt, pepper and curry powder
